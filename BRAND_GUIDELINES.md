# SMG Transport Agency Brand Guidelines

## Brand Position

SMG Transport Agency is a youth-driven Ghanaian transport brand focused on reliable, comfortable and transparent travel.

## Tagline

**Your Journey. Your Seat. Your Time.**

Use the tagline on hero sections, social previews, launch materials and branded presentations. Do not rewrite it casually; keep the exact punctuation.

## Color Palette

| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---:|---|
| Travel Blue | `#4F9BE8` | Primary brand field, favicon background, energetic accents |
| Deep Navy | `#003366` | Headers, navigation, footer, primary text, high-trust surfaces |
| Warm Peach | `#F7B267` | Logo mark, key accents, highlights, badges, focus states |
| Cloud Grey | `#F5F7FA` | Soft page bands, neutral backgrounds, empty states |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Main page background and text on dark surfaces |

## Typography

- Headings: **Montserrat**, weights 600, 700 and 800.
- Body: **Open Sans**, weights 400, 500 and 600.
- Use sentence case for most UI labels.
- Avoid negative letter spacing and oversized text inside cards or admin panels.

## Logo System

- Primary logo asset: `public/brand/smg-logo.png`.
- Mark asset: `public/brand/logo-mark.png`.
- Favicon assets: `public/brand/favicon-32.png`, `public/brand/favicon-96.png`, `public/brand/apple-touch-icon.png`, `public/brand/icon-192.png`, `public/brand/icon-512.png`.
- Social preview: `public/brand/social-share.png`.

Logo rules:

- Keep the bus mark in Warm Peach on Travel Blue or a clean light background.
- Leave clear space around the mark equal to at least half the mark height.
- Do not stretch, skew, rotate or recolor the logo.
- Do not place the logo over busy photography without a solid backing.
- Do not add shadows, outlines or extra slogans inside the logo artwork.

## Photography

- Fleet photography should feel clean, safe, modern and Ghana-relevant.
- Prefer daylight, real terminals, roads, coach interiors and service details.
- Avoid crowded scenes, readable fake brand text, distorted buses, harsh filters and low-light images.
- Do not add team photos unless approved separately.

## Voice And Tone

- Clear, calm and helpful.
- Confident without sounding corporate-heavy.
- Use plain travel language: book, choose your seat, pay, board, manage booking.
- Avoid overpromising exact arrival times; use transparent policy language for delays and changes.

## UI Rules

- Use Deep Navy for trust and structure.
- Use Warm Peach sparingly for action emphasis, icon accents and highlight moments.
- Keep admin screens dense, practical and easy to scan.
- Public booking screens should feel warm, mobile-first and direct.
